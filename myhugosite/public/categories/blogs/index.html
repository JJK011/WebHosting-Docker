Space Isn't Just for Scientists-It's for You Too!

About 4.5 billion years ago, the planets of our solar system, including Earth, were formed. Life began about 3.5 billion years ago. But was the origin of life something that happened deep within Earth itself, or did a comet or asteroid carrying the seeds of life crash onto Earth by chance? That still remains a mystery.

Human evolution began roughly 5 million years ago, but it took nearly 4.9 million years after that before the first real human settlements appeared. Those early hunters gradually became aware of their surroundings, learning how to use the environment for their development.
